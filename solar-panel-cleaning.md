## Process

### Step 1: Assessment
- Count panels
- Check access
- Note any damage
- Photo documentation

### Step 2: Pre-Spray
- Apply cleaning solution
- Let dwell (2-5 min)

### Step 3: Brush & Rinse
- Use water-fed pole (no walking on roof!)
- Brush from top down
- Rinse with pure water (DI)

### Step 4: Post-Clean
- Final rinse
- Before/after photos
- Efficiency check if possible

### Step 5: Invoice
- Send photo report
- Offer maintenance plan

## Equipment Details

### Water-Fed Pole System
- **Telescopic pole:** 12-24 ft reach
- **Brush head:** 10-18 inches
- **Water feed:** Internal pole (keeps you dry)

### Deionized (DI) Water
- **Why:** No mineral spots after drying
- **System:** DI resin filter + pump
- **Usage:** ~50-100 gallons per job

### Cleaning Solution
- **Type:** Solar-specific or mild detergent
- **Eco-friendly** options available
- **Never** use abrasive materials
