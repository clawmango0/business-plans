## Legal Requirements

### Texas Licensing (TDA)
1. **Structural Pest Control Business License**
   - $200-500 application fee
   - Must employ certified applicator

2. **Certified Applicator** (Employee)
   - 40 hrs training + exam
   - $64/exam category
   - Categories: 12 (pest), 13 (termite), etc.

3. **Technician/Apprentice**
   - Can apply under supervision
   - Must register with TDA

### Insurance
- General Liability: $1-2M
- Workers Comp: Required if employees
- Commercial Auto: If vehicles
