## Executive Summary
- **Business Name:** [TBD - "FTW Event Rentals" or "Cowtown Party Supply"]

## Operations

### Inventory (Initial)
- 100 folding chairs
- 20 banquet tables
- 10 round tables
- 4 tents (various sizes)
- Basic tabletop (per event)
- Delivery van

### Delivery & Setup
- Offer delivery + setup for $50–$150 (distance-based)
- Pickup available (customer responsibility)
- Setup assistance: $25/hour per person

### Rental Process
1. Customer selects items online or by phone
2. Request deposit (25% to hold)
3. Delivery/pickup day
4. Event
5. Pickup/return
6. Damage assessment + final payment

### Damage Policy
- Security deposit: $100–$500 (varies by items)
- Replacement cost for damages
- Cleaning fee: $25–$50 if not returned clean
