## Legal & Tax

### Structure
- LLC (protect personal assets)
- Commercial auto insurance (REQUIRED)
- Sales tax collection (Texas motor vehicle rental tax: 10%)

### Insurance
- Commercial liability: $1M minimum
- Physical damage: Comprehensive + collision
- Require customers to have own coverage OR purchase rental coverage ($15–$25/day)

### Tax
- Section 179 for car purchases (up to certain limits)
- Depreciation: 5-year MACRS
- Operating expenses: Fuel, insurance, maintenance, washing
- QBI deduction
