## Operations

### Equipment List
- Pressure washer (gas preferred)
- Shop vacuum + attachments
- Steam cleaner
- Dual-action polisher
- Detailing brushes (various sizes)
- Microfiber towels (100+)
- Leather cleaning kit
- Clay bar kit
- pH-balanced cleaners
- Tire cleaner + dressing

### Workflow
1. Arrive at customer location
2. Pre-inspection (document existing damage)
3. Exterior wash + wheels
4. Interior vacuum + surfaces
5. Deep clean (as needed)
6. Final inspection
7. Collect payment

### Schedule
- Monday: Admin/marketing
- Tuesday–Saturday: Jobs
- Sunday: Off (or emergency only)
