# Ceramic Coating Business Plan

## Executive Summary
- **Business Name:** [TBD - "FTW Ceramic Pro" or "Lone Star Coating"]
- **Type:** Mobile Ceramic Coating & Paint Protection
- **Location:** Fort Worth, Texas (mobile service)
- **Mission:** Provide professional ceramic coating services at customer locations with premium results

## Market Opportunity
- Fort Worth car culture is growing (trucks, sports cars, exotics)
- Ceramic coating is $500–$1,500 per vehicle — huge margins
- Mobile = no shop overhead
- Recurring: Coatings last 2–5 years, customers return
- DIY kits exist but pros dominate quality work

## Services Offered

### Coating Packages
| Package | Price | What's Included |
|---------|-------|-----------------|
| Express | $300–$400 | 1-year coating, single panel or full car (small cars) |
| Standard | $500–$700 | 3-year coating, full car exterior |
| Premium | $800–$1,200 | 5-year coating, full exterior + wheels |
| Ultimate | $1,500–$2,500 | 7-year coating, full exterior + wheels + glass + interior |

- Paint correction (remove swirls/scratches): $300–$800

- Interior leather coating: $150–$250

### Maintenance Packages
- Annual refresh: $100–$150

## Operations

### Process (Standard Coating)
1. **Wash & Decontaminate** — 30–60 min
2. **Paint Correction** (if needed) — 1–4 hours
3. **Surface Prep** — 30 min
4. **Apply Coating** — 1–2 hours
5. **Cure Time** — Allow 24 hours before exposure
6. **Final Inspection** — 15 min

### Time Requirements
- Express: 3–4 hours
- Standard: 5–6 hours
- Premium: 7–8 hours
- Ultimate: 10–12 hours

### Equipment Needed
- Dual-action polisher
- Coating applicator pads
- Microfiber towels (50+)
- Spray bottles
- pH-neutral car shampoo
- Iron remover
- Clay bar kit
- LED inspection light
- Respirator + ventilation
