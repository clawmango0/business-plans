# E-Bike Rental Business Plan

## Executive Summary
- **Business Name:** [TBD - "FTW eBikes" or "Cowtown Cycles"]
- **Type:** Electric Bike Rental + Tours
- **Location:** Fort Worth, Texas
- **Mission:** Provide fun, eco-friendly e-bike rentals and tours in Fort Worth

## Market Opportunity
- Fort Worth has improving bike infrastructure (Trinity River Trails, Downtown)
- Tourist interest in "Cowtown" culture
- TCU students and staff
- Corporate team-building events
- Weekend recreational riders
- NOT Austin — manage expectations

## Financial Projections

### Startup Costs

| Safety gear (helmets) | $300–$500 |

| eBikes trailer/van | $3,000–$8,000 |
| Insurance (general liability) | $500–$1,000/year |
| Marketing + website | $500–$1,000 |
| **Total Startup** | **$19,500–$35,900** |

### Monthly Expenses
| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Insurance | $50–$100 |
| Maintenance (battery, tires, brakes) | $200–$400 |
| Marketing | $100–$200 |
| Storage (if needed) | $0–$300 |
| Vehicle/gas | $100–$200 |
| **Total Monthly** | **$450–$1,200** |

### Revenue Projections
- **Season (March–November):** Higher demand
- **Off-season (Dec–Feb):** Lower demand
- **Assumption:** 5 rentals/day average (season), 2/day (off)
- **Daily avg:** 3.5 rentals × $50 = $175/day
- **Monthly (season):** 22 days × $175 = $3,850
- **Monthly (off):** 22 days × $100 = $2,200
- **Annual:** ~$33,000 revenue
- **Net Profit:** $20,000–$28,000/year

## Operations

### Bike Maintenance
- Battery care: Charge after each use, store at 50%
- Regular service: Every 50–100 hours of use
- Clean + tune monthly
- Replace tires every 1,000 miles

### Weather Policy
- Rain: No rentals (safety)
- Heat: Early morning/evening only (May–September)
- Winter: Reduced hours

### Seasonal Strategy
- Spring: Heavy marketing for tourist season
- Summer: Early morning tours only
- Fall: Peak season
- Winter: Maintenance, planning, corporate sales

## Legal & Tax

### Structure
- LLC recommended

### Insurance
- General liability: $1M
- Business property (bikes covered separately)
- Waiver for customers

### Regulations
- Fort Worth: No special license for bike rental
- Trail access: Some trails require permits
- Helmet laws: Texas doesn't require, but recommend

### Tax
- Section 179 for bikes
- QBI deduction
- Track all expenses

## Risk Factors
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Theft | Security deposit ($200), lock bikes, track via AirTag |
| Injury | Waiver, helmet policy, safety briefing |
| Weather | Weather-dependent booking |
