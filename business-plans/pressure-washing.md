# Pressure Washing Business Plan

## Executive Summary
- **Business Name:** [TBD - "Fort Worth Pressure Wash" or similar]
- **Type:** Mobile Pressure Washing Service
- **Location:** Fort Worth, Texas (mobile, service all of Tarrant County)
- **Mission:** Provide residential and commercial pressure washing services with reliability, quality, and fair pricing

## Market Opportunity
- Fort Worth has 1M+ homes with concrete, driveways, fences, and siding
- High humidity = mold/algae growth = recurring cleaning demand
- HOA communities require exterior maintenance
- Low competition from professional crews (mostly solo operators)

## Operations

### Equipment Needed
- Gas pressure washer (4,000–5,000 PSI)
- Surface cleaner (for concrete)
- Extension wands
- Chemical injection system
- 50-ft hose minimum
- PPE: safety glasses, gloves, boots

### Schedule (Typical)
- Monday: Marketing/Admin
- Tuesday–Saturday: Jobs
- Sunday: Off

### Weather Constraints
- Cannot operate in rain or electrical storms
- Summer heat: Early morning starts (6 AM) to avoid midday heat
- Winter: Reduced demand, maintenance season

## Legal & Tax

### Structure
- Recommended: Single-member LLC (protect personal assets)
- Register with Texas Secretary of State ($300)
- Get EIN ($0, IRS free)

### Insurance
- General Liability: $500–$1,000/year ($1M coverage)
- Workers comp: Not required for solo (but recommended if hiring)

### Tax Deductions
- Section 179: Full deduction for equipment in year purchased
- Vehicle: Standard mileage OR actual expenses
- Home office: If operating from home
- Phone, marketing, supplies: Business expense

## Competitor Analysis

### Local Competitors
- **Texas Pressure Washing** — $200–$400 average, good reviews
- **Fort Worth Soft Wash** — Focuses on roofs, higher prices
- **Handyman services** — Often subpar, opportunity to differentiate

### Differentiation Strategy
- Response time (same-day or next-day vs. 1-week wait)
- Professional appearance (uniformed, branded truck)
- Before/after documentation
- Online booking + easy payment
- Satisfaction guarantee
