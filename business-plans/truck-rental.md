# Truck Rental Business Plan (Work Truck Niche)

## Executive Summary
- **Business Name:** [TBD - "WorkTruck FXW" or "Truck Substitute"]
- **Type:** Short-term Work Truck Rental
- **Location:** Fort Worth, Texas (serving DFW metro)
- **Mission:** Provide reliable temporary work truck rentals for contractors, tradespeople, and homeowners when their primary vehicle is unavailable

## Market Opportunity
- DFW has 50,000+ construction/trade contractors
- Fleet vehicles break down; body shop repairs take weeks
- Homeowners need trucks for weekend projects
- Traditional rental companies don't cater to "work truck" market
- Turo has trucks but inconsistent, high fees

## Services Offered

### Core Rentals
| Vehicle Type | Daily Rate | Weekly Rate | Use Case |
|-------------|------------|-------------|----------|
| Full-Size Pickup (F-150, Silverado 1500) | $80–$100 | $400–$500 | General work, hauling |
| Extended Cab Pickup | $90–$110 | $450–$550 | Crew + equipment |
| Cargo Van (Sprinter, ProMaster) | $100–$130 | $500–$650 | Tools, deliveries |
| With Trailer | +$25–$50/day | +$150/week | Extra capacity |

### Add-On Services
- Delivery/pickup: $25 (within 20 miles)
- Dolly/cargo trailer: $25/day
- Moving blankets + tie-downs: $15
- GPS rental: $10/day
- Pre-paid fuel (full tank): $50–$70

### Insurance Options
- Customer provides own: Verify coverage
- Basic liability included: $10/day
- Premium (full coverage): $25/day
- Waiver (reduce deductible): $15/day

### Monthly Expenses
| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Commercial auto insurance | $1,000–$1,500 |
| Truck payment (if financed) | $1,200–$1,800 |
| Maintenance/reserves | $300–$500 |
| Marketing | $200–$400 |
| Phone + tools | $100 |
| **Total Monthly** | **$2,800–$4,300** |

### Revenue Projections
- **Conservative (50% utilization):** 15 rented days/truck/month × 3 trucks × $90/day = $4,050/month
- **Moderate (65% utilization):** 20 days × 3 × $90 = $5,400/month
- **Aggressive (80% utilization):** 24 days × 3 × $90 = $6,480/month
- **Net Profit:** $1,200–$3,700/month (early years, building equity in trucks)

### Break-Even Analysis
- Fixed costs: ~$3,000/month
- At $90/day: Need 33 rented days/month to break even
- With 3 trucks: 11 days per truck = 37% utilization

## Legal & Tax

### Structure
- LLC (single-member or partnership)
- Separate commercial auto policy (REQUIRED)
- Personal auto policy will NOT cover commercial rental

### Insurance Requirements
- Commercial auto: $1M liability minimum
- Comprehensive/collision on each truck
- Uninsured motorist coverage
- Get quotes from: Progressive Commercial, GEICO Business, State Farm

### Tax Treatment
- Section 179: Deduct truck purchase (if <116K each)
- Depreciation: MACRS 5-year schedule
- Operating expenses: Fuel, insurance, maintenance
- QBI deduction: 20% of net income
