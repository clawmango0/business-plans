## The "Hired Applicator" Model

### How It Works
- **You:** CEO, handle sales, marketing, operations, scheduling
- **Employee:** Licensed Certified Applicator ($25-40/hr or $65-75K/yr)
- **Technician:** Helper ($15-25/hr)

### This is LEGAL in Texas!
- Business needs Responsible Certified Applicator on staff
- YOU don't need to be licensed
- HIRE someone with the license

### What You Need
- Business License: ~$300
- Responsible Certified Applicator (employee)
- General Liability Insurance

## Texas Licensing Requirements

### Business License
- Structural Pest Control Business License
- ~$300 fee
- Must employ Certified Applicator

### Employee Licensing
- **Technician:** $125 + $64 exam
- **Certified Applicator:** $125 + $64/exam category
- **Category 12:** General Pest Control (most important)

### Timeline
- Apply: 1-2 weeks
- Training: 40 hours
- Exam: Can schedule after training
- **Total: 4-8 weeks to get licensed employee**
