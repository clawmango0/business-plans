## Operations

### Licensing (Texas)
- **General Liability:** Required ($1-2M)
- **Workers Comp:** Required if employees
- **DOT:** If truck >10,000 lbs GVWR
- **LLC:** Recommended for liability

### Equipment Needed
- Dollys (4+)
- Furniture straps
- Tarps
- Gloves, safety gear
- First aid kit

### Landfill Costs (Tarrant County)
- ~$45/ton average
- Check: Republic Services, Waste Management
- Alternative: Donation centers (FREE for furniture)

### Typical Job Timeline
1. Phone/online booking (10 min)
2. Arrival & assessment (5 min)
3. Loading (30-60 min)
4. Hauling (15-30 min)
5. Unloading (15-30 min)
6. Return/next job (15 min)
- **Total: 1.5-2.5 hours/job**

## Financial Projections

### Conservative (Part-Time)
- 2 jobs/week × $200 avg = $400/week
- $1,600/month revenue
- Expenses: $600 (fuel, disposal, insurance)
- **NET: $1,000/month**

### Moderate (Full-Time Solo)
- 4 jobs/week × $225 avg = $900/week
- $3,600/month revenue
- Expenses: $1,200 (fuel, disposal, insurance, marketing)
- **NET: $2,400/month**
- **Year 1: $28,800**

### Scaling (With Helper)
- 6 jobs/week × $250 avg = $1,500/week
- $6,000/month revenue
- Helper: $800
- Expenses: $1,500
- **NET: $3,700/month**
- **Year 1: $44,400**

### Break-Even
- ~15 jobs/month at $200 avg
- Covers: truck payment, insurance, fuel, disposal

## Tax Strategies

### Section 179 (Vehicle)
- Write off vehicle cost in Year 1
- Up to $2.5M (phased out over $4M)
- Trucks over 6,000 lbs qualify

### LLC Structure
- Single-member LLC (disregarded entity)
- Pass-through taxation
- S-Corp election at $80K+ revenue (save SE tax)

### Quarterly Estimated Taxes
- Due: April 15, June 15, Sept 15, Jan 15
- Estimate using Form 1040-ES
- Penalty if underpaid

### Deductible Expenses
- Vehicle (mileage or actual)
- Fuel
- Insurance
- Marketing
- Phone
- Tools/equipment
