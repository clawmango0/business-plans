## Fiverr Economics

### Fees
- Fiverr takes 20%
- You keep 80%

### Price Examples
| Gig Price | You Receive |
|-----------|-------------|
| $15 | $12 |
| $35 | $28 |
| $75 | $60 |
| $150 | $120 |

### To Make $5,000/month
- $75 gigs: 67/month = ~2/day
- $150 gigs: 42/month = ~1.5/day

## Key Insights from Research

### Winning Formula
1. **Niche down** - "SOP for [specific industry]" > generic
2. **Professional look** - Canva, clean design
3. **Clear instructions** - Buyers need help using
4. **Responsive** - Fast delivery, quick responses
5. **Reviews** - Build credibility

### Avoid
- $5 gigs (race to bottom)
- Generic titles
- Poor grammar/design
- Slow delivery
