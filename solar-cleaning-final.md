## Operations

### Equipment Details
- **Water-fed pole:** 12-24 ft reach
- **DI (Deionized) water:** Prevents water spots
- **Brush head:** 10-18 inches
- **No walking on panels** (prevents damage!)

### Process
1. Pre-spray with cleaning solution
2. Brush with water-fed pole
3. Rinse with DI water
4. Photo documentation
5. Invoice + offer maintenance plan

### Best Seasons (Texas)
- **Peak:** March-May (post-winter, pre-summer)
- **Good:** September-October (pre-winter)
- **Avoid:** Summer heat (midday), Winter (rare demand)

### Weather Considerations
- Not when raining
- Not when too hot (solution dries)
- Best: Overcast morning

### Time Per Job
- Small (15 panels): 30-45 min
- Standard (25 panels): 45-60 min
- Large (40 panels): 90-120 min
