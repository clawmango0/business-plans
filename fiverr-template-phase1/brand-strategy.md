## üèÜ RECOMMENDATION: **TemplateForge**

**Why:**
- Professional but approachable
- Works across all 6 categories
- Easy to remember
- Available domain potential (templateforge.com)
- "Forge" implies building quality

**Alternative:** BizStack if targeting tech/startup crowd

## Positioning

- **Target:** Busy small business owners who need professional documents
- **Value:** Save time, look professional, done-for-you quality
- **Differentiation:** Industry-specific bundles vs generic templates

---

## Visual Direction

- **Colors:** Navy blue (professional), Green (growth/success), White (clean)
- **Vibe:** Clean, modern, efficient
- **Style:** Minimalist, not cluttered
